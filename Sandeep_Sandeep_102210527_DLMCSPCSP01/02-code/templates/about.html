Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by going to the login page and clicking on the "Forgot Password?" link.

How can I delete my account?

If you wish to delete your account, please contact our support team through the contact form available on our support page.

Can I sync my tasks across devices?

Yes, Task Master supports syncing across multiple devices to ensure that your tasks are always up-to-date, no matter where you access your account from.
